Meet the S-Cube

It'll blow your mind

The S-Cube features six different fidgeting tools, each aiming to satisfy a different fidgeting need. With your thumb, you might enjoy the switch or if you are a gamer, you'll feel right at home with a very ergonomical joy stick. If you are a worrier, the end to your anxiety has come; simply roll your thumb over the specially designed indent and practice your breathing to relax.

It's that good

If you prefer rolling instead, we have what you need. A simple roller-ball that is clickable and three mini gears are more than satisfying. Alternatively, you could also try the spinner on the opposite side, which is just as pleasurable.

{% include picture.html name="rollers.jpg" class="img-responsive center-block" alt="S-Cube - Roll!" %}

See for yourself

Finally, if you are addicted to that satisfying pen clicking, no need to carry that pen around. The S-Cube has a specifically designed side featuring 5 pen-clicking simulation buttons that are ever more satisfying than a real pen. And if you are in a meeting, we've got you covered - two of them are silent.
