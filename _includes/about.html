About us

Multiplex is a company that was established by 9 aspiring English School students.

From the start, we became very interested in finding a solution to the problem of stress, something that almost everyone faces nowadays. This was the beginning of the s-cube, a smart gadget that aims to reduce stress by providing things to keep your hands busy with. Each of its six sides has a different tool for you to play with, from pen-clicking simulation buttons to an analogue stick and a roller ball.

This gadget not only reduces stress through a fun way; it is also easy to carry around, since it is small and light weight, a perfect all day companion!
